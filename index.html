Clip & share your testimony

Every committee hearing is recorded and public record. You spoke up — now make it travel. Find your moment, clip it, and post it with a caption that's already written for you.

1
Find your hearing video House committee streams are archived on the House video archive (hosted on YouTube) · Senate hearings live in the Senate video archive. Search by committee name and meeting date.
2
Mark your moment Scrub to where the chair calls your name. Note your start and end time below — most testimony runs 1–3 minutes.
3
Clip it On YouTube videos: hit Share → Clip to cut up to 60 seconds with zero editing skills. For longer moments or Senate videos: screen-record the segment (Mac: Cmd+Shift+5 · iPhone: Control Center → Screen Recording). It's public record — you're allowed.
4
Post it with a caption below Tag your legislators. Decision-makers absolutely see this.
