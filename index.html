Clip & share your moment

Every hearing is recorded and public. You spoke up — now let it travel. Find your moment, clip it, and post it with a caption that's already written.

1
Find your hearing video Senate hearings are streamed and saved on the Senate of Virginia YouTube channel · House meetings are archived on the House meeting schedule. Search by committee name and meeting date.
2
Mark your moment Scrub to where the chair calls your name. Note your start and end time below — most testimony runs 1–3 minutes.
3
Clip it On YouTube: hit Share → Clip to cut up to 60 seconds, no editing skills needed. For longer moments: screen-record the segment (Mac: Cmd+Shift+5 · iPhone: Control Center → Screen Recording). It's public record — you're allowed.
4
Post it with a caption below Tag your legislators. They absolutely see this.
