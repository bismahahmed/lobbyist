# LobbyistIQ

**Know what's coming. Know what to do.**

Legislative intelligence for people who aren't lobbyists — small businesses, nonprofits, and trade associations that can't afford one.

- Live ticker and breaking alerts when a bill that affects you moves
- Impact scores and plain-English briefs: what it costs you and when
- Action templates: who to contact and exactly what to say
- Coordination with others in your industry fighting the same bills

*Prototype — demo data only. Bill and delegate information shown is illustrative and should not be relied on.*
